TIMEPLANNER


MANAGING YOUR TIME THE RIGHT WAY

Wasting time in meetings is an important factor for most companies.In the last 20 years, the number of meetings has almost doubled, 67% of workers say spending too much time in meetings distracts them from doing their job. The cost of poorly organised meetings is $399 billion in the US in 2019 alone.

  • October 2020

  • Project Description

    TimePlanner is an application that manages meetings more efficiently. The application will be able to check if the respective meeting is needed and setting the level of need and if the persons involved are suitable for the meeting, using artificial intelligence based on the history of existing meetings. It will have a list of points to discuss for the respective meeting and the time allocated to each point, at the end there will be a feedback from the participants for the respective meeting which will be taken into account at the next meetings.
